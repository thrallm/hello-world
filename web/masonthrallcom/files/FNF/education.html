Composting
Date: TBA, early Summer

Experiential education on the dynamics of composting in your backyard. We will be making a small compost pile in preparation for the fall nutrition needs of our gardens and in hopes that others might be inspired to divert their food and garden waste in a productive manner. Why? In accordance with our mission, we seek to reduce the food-based carbon emissions of our community and thus we are reaching out to disseminate knowledge on an excellent waster diversion practice that can also serve as a natural fertilizer.

Cost? Donations accepted but not required.
