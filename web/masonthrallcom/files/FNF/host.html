Interested in letting us grow healthy food in your yard?

In preparation for the 2010 growing season, we are seeking host properties in the Flatirons Neighborhood.

Some of the benefits of donating all or part of your yard to Flatirons Neighborhood Farm:
  • We will design, install, plant, maintain, and harvest a synthetic fertilizer and pesticide-free garden of vegetables, herbs and flowers in your yard. We will supply the labor, tools, seeds and starts required to transform your yard into a flourishing and interconnected part of our community.
  • Hosts will have access to a limited amount of self-harvested produce. We ask that hosts do not use more than can consumed daily.
  • Hosts will receive a discount on a CSA share.
